## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

PathPilotIndia is a sophisticated AI-powered career guidance platform specifically designed for the Indian education ecosystem. The platform serves students in Classes 8-12 and their parents, providing personalized career path recommendations, college matching algorithms, and comprehensive scholarship information.

### Core Mission
To democratize career guidance in India by leveraging artificial intelligence to provide accessible, personalized, and data-driven career counseling that helps students make informed decisions about their academic and professional futures.
